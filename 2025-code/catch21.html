How to Play

  1. Cast your line to catch fish weighing 0, 1, 3, or 5 lbs.
  2. Try to get as close to 21 lbs as possible without going over.
  3. If your total exceeds 21 lbs, you capsize the boat!
  4. Dock to end your fishing trip early and save your score.
