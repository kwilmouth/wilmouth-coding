How to Play

  1. Take turns growing, selling, or buying potatoes each week.
    • Grow. Potatoes take a week to grow from seeds to full potatoes. Test you luck with earning either a bountiful harvest or a withered one.
    • Sell. Sell all your potatoes for more coins. This will allow you to buy seeds to earn more at the end of the season.
    • Buy. Buy the max amount of seeds that your coins will allow and keep planting.
  2. Grow the most potatoes to win.
