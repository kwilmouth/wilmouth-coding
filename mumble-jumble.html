How to Play

  1. Read the gibberish phrase out loud and try to guess what real phrase it sounds like.
  2. Stuck? Click “Show Hint” for a helpful clue.
  3. Type your guess in the input box and click “Submit Answer.”
  4. If your guess is correct, you'll see “Correct!” and the “Skip” button becomes “Next Phrase.” If it's wrong, you'll see “Try Again”.
  5. Click “Next Phrase” or “Skip” to load a new card.
  6. Keep going and enjoy the game for as long as you like!
